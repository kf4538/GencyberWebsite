2024 GenCyber Summer All Girls Camp

July 15 - July 19

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there any cost to attend?
    This event is funded entirely by the NSA and there is no cost to the students.
  • My child has no computing experience, can they still participate?
    Yes! Materials are designed for participants without computing abilities.
  • Is lunch provided?
    Lunch is provided at an RIT dining hall.
  • What are the camp hours?
    The camp runs from 9am to 4pm with drop off as early as 8:30am.
  • Is this an all girls camp?
    While open to all eligible participants regardless of race or gender, female-identifying and gender-minority-identifying students are encouraged to register!
